# Ibn Al Haytham

1. Shukūk (Methodological Doubt) > Surah Yunus (10:94): doubts should lead to inquiry, Surah Al-Isra (17:36): rejection of unverified authoritative claims, (2:260): productive doubt aimed at reaching certainty.
2. I’tibār (Controlled Experimentation) >  29:20 and 10:101: active observation, (3:190-191): reflect on the creation, Hadith scholarship
3. Burhān (Mathematical Proof) > (2:111, 27:64): bring your Burhan, (4:174): Burhān is a clear, manifest sign, based on self-evident axioms, kind of like mathematical proofs
